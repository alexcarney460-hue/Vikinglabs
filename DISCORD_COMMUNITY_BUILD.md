## ğŸ¯ Community Guidelines (Full Version)

### **Viking Labs Discord Community Guidelines**

**Our Commitment**
We're building a space for researchers, enthusiasts, and partners to discuss peptide science, share knowledge, and support each other. We're committed to quality, respect, and learning.

**Core Rules**

1. **Be Respectful**
   - No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
   - Disagreement is good. Personal attacks are not.
   - Assume good intent

2. **No Medical/Financial Advice**
   - We're a research community, not medical professionals
   - Don't prescribe dosages or treatments
   - Don't promise health outcomes
   - Share research, not claims
   - Direct medical questions to healthcare providers

3. **No Spam or Self-Promotion**
   - No external referral links without approval
   - No repeated cross-posting
   - No MLM or pyramid scheme promotion
   - Approved partners can promote in designated channels

4. **Stay On-Topic**
   - #general: casual chat welcome
   - #peptide-science: research only
   - #stack-discussion: protocols/education only
   - Off-topic content goes to #off-topic

5. **No Illegal Content**
   - No illegal substances discussion
   - No copyright infringement
   - No personal doxxing
   - Report to Discord/law enforcement if serious

6. **Respect Testing & Feedback**
   - Lab testing feedback is welcome
   - Constructive criticism encouraged
   - No false reviews
   - No defamation

**Consequences**
- 1st violation: Warning + explanation
- 2nd violation: Mute 24-72 hours
- 3rd violation: Kick from server
- Severe (harassment, hate speech): Immediate ban

**Reporting**
See something wrong? DM @Moderator or @Admin. We read all reports.
