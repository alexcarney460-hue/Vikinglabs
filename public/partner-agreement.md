# VIKING LABS PARTNER COMMISSION SCALE & AGREEMENT

**Version: 1.0**

---

## 1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The Viking Labs Partner Program rewards approved affiliates, influencers, and referral partners based on verified referred sales. This commission structure is designed to:

- Provide scalable earning opportunities for partners
- Maintain long-term program stability
- Ensure fair and transparent attribution
- Protect against fraud, abuse, and misuse

**Net Referred Revenue** is defined as successfully captured payments minus refunds, chargebacks, reversals, and invalid transactions.

---

## 2. COMMISSION TIERS

Partner commission rates are determined by rolling 30-day referred revenue performance.

### TIER 1 — STARTER PARTNER
- **Monthly Referred Revenue:** $10,000 – $24,999
- **Commission Rate:** 10%
- **Benefits:**
  - Unique affiliate tracking link
  - Unique partner discount code
  - Standard customer discount eligibility
  - Standard monthly payout cycle

### TIER 2 — GROWTH PARTNER
- **Monthly Referred Revenue:** $25,000 – $74,999
- **Commission Rate:** 14%
- **Additional Benefits:**
  - Increased commission rate
  - Eligibility for promotional incentives
  - Standard monthly payout cycle

### TIER 3 — SCALE PARTNER
- **Monthly Referred Revenue:** $75,000 – $149,999
- **Commission Rate:** 18%
- **Additional Benefits:**
  - Enhanced earnings structure
  - Discount flexibility up to approved limits
  - Eligibility for bonus campaigns
  - Standard monthly payout cycle

### TIER 4 — ELITE PARTNER
- **Monthly Referred Revenue:** $150,000 – $249,999
- **Commission Rate:** 21%
- **Additional Benefits:**
  - Priority support consideration
  - Custom landing page eligibility
  - Quarterly bonus eligibility
  - Inventory priority consideration
  - **NET-7 PAYOUT ELIGIBILITY** (subject to approval)

### TIER 5 — APEX PARTNER
- **Monthly Referred Revenue:** $250,000+
- **Commission Rate:** 23%
- **Additional Benefits:**
  - Maximum commission tier
  - Custom promotional opportunities
  - Exclusive campaign eligibility
  - Highest payout priority
  - **NET-7 PAYOUT ELIGIBILITY** (subject to approval)

---

## 3. REVENUE ATTRIBUTION

Commissions are credited exclusively on qualifying purchases made through:
- Approved affiliate tracking links OR
- Approved partner discount codes

Viking Labs reserves the right to apply last-touch or blended attribution models where necessary. All attribution determinations are final.

---

## 4. COMMISSION CALCULATION

Commissions are based on verified Net Referred Revenue only.

**Excluded from commission eligibility:**
- Refunded transactions
- Chargebacks
- Fraudulent or suspicious transactions
- Reversed payments
- Self-referred purchases
- Invalid or manipulated orders

Viking Labs reserves the right to adjust commissions retroactively.

---

## 5. PAYOUT TERMS

**Standard payout cycle:** Monthly

**Accelerated payout eligibility:**
- Tier 4 (Elite Partner) → Eligible for Net-7 payouts upon approval
- Tier 5 (Apex Partner) → Eligible for Net-7 payouts upon approval

Net-7 payouts are not automatic and require:
- Account standing review
- Traffic quality validation
- Fraud-risk assessment

Viking Labs reserves the right to revoke accelerated payouts at any time. Commissions are not considered earned until transactions are successfully verified and cleared.

---

## 6. DISCOUNT CODE POLICY

**Default customer discount eligibility:**
- Tier 1 – Tier 2 → Standard discount level
- Tier 3+ → Expanded discount eligibility (approval required)

Unauthorized discounting or code misuse is prohibited. Improper discount practices may result in commission reversals or termination from the program.

---

## 7. PARTNER AGREEMENT & TERMS

Participation in the Viking Labs Partner Program constitutes acceptance of the following terms:

### 7.1 Compliance & Representation

Partners agree to:
- Provide truthful, non-misleading promotional content
- Avoid medical or therapeutic claims
- Comply with applicable advertising regulations
- Follow platform-specific policies
- Represent Viking Labs professionally

### 7.2 Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:
- Self-referrals or indirect self-purchases
- Misleading or deceptive promotions
- Unauthorized advertising methods
- Fraudulent transactions
- Artificial traffic generation
- Misrepresentation of products or policies

Violations may result in commission reversals or removal.

### 7.3 Abuse Prevention & Program Integrity

To maintain fairness and program stability, the following rules apply:

#### A. Self-Referral Restrictions
Commissions will not be paid on purchases made by:
- The partner
- Associated persons or employees
- Controlled or duplicate accounts
- Any party acting on behalf of the partner

Viking Labs reserves sole authority to determine self-referral patterns.

#### B. Discount & Coupon Abuse
**Prohibited behaviors include:**
- Publishing codes on coupon aggregation websites
- Misrepresenting codes as public offers
- Unauthorized discount stacking
- Artificial conversion manipulation

**Improper discount usage may trigger:**
- Commission reversals
- Tier downgrades
- Program termination

#### C. Paid Advertising & Brand Bidding
Partners may not run paid advertisements using:
- Viking Labs brand name
- Trademarked product names
- Brand variations or misspellings
- Direct-link ads without approval

#### D. Fraudulent or Manipulated Transactions
Commissions are void for transactions involving:
- Stolen payment methods
- Refund manipulation
- Fake or test orders
- Artificial traffic
- Any activity deemed abusive or fraudulent

Viking Labs may reverse earnings retroactively.

#### E. Traffic Quality Standards
Partners must not generate traffic via:
- Bots or automation systems
- Spam or unsolicited messaging
- Forced clicks or redirects
- Incentivized purchases without approval

Traffic must be legitimate and compliant.

#### F. Order Validation & Adjustments
All commissions remain pending until validated. Viking Labs may adjust or revoke commissions due to:
- Refunds
- Chargebacks
- Payment reversals
- Fraud detection
- Policy violations

All adjustments are final.

### 7.4 Commission Integrity

Viking Labs reserves the right to reverse commissions resulting from:
- Invalid transactions
- Fraud indicators
- Abuse patterns
- Policy violations

All commission decisions are final.

### 7.5 Payment & Liability Limitations

- Earnings are performance-based and not guaranteed
- Viking Labs is not liable for indirect losses
- Commission eligibility requires full compliance

### 7.6 Suspension & Termination Rights

Viking Labs may suspend or terminate participation at any time for:
- Fraud or abuse risk
- Policy violations
- Brand protection concerns
- Unacceptable promotional practices

Unpaid commissions may be forfeited at Viking Labs discretion.

### 7.7 Program Modifications

Viking Labs reserves the right to modify:
- Commission rates
- Tier thresholds
- Program rules
- Partner benefits

Continued participation constitutes acceptance of updates.
